Loading Your License

Loading your license

  1. After you have purchased App Stop you should recieve a confirmation email containing your license as a attachment.
  2. If you have not recieved a confirmation email, check your spam box, sometimes it gets marked as spam. If you are still unable to locate the email contact me for additional support.
  3. Save the license file to your desktop
  4. Click 'Load License' menu-item under the 'App Stop' menu (as shown below)
  5. From the file-browser choose your license file from the desktop
  6. That's it! A panel should pop-up notifying you that you have licensed App Stop. Thank you for supporting shareware!

If you are having trouble loading your license

  1. Check the size of your license file, if it is '0kb' it is corrupted
  2. Sometimes web-mail can cause the license file to be downloaded incorrectly, if you retrieved your license file via web-mail try using a different method
  3. If you are still experiencing difficulty contact support
